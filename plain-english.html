Plain English Overview

This app is a personal helper for your health goals.

You set the goal. The app helps you decide, book, pay, and track in one place.

What It Does

Simple user journey

You tell it what you want

  • More energy
  • Lose fat
  • Better sleep
  • Live longer

Then it helps you

  • Choose the right tests, treatments, supplements, and clinics
  • Book them in one place
  • Pay in one place
  • Track your results in one place

Why users care

  • Saves time
  • Removes guesswork
  • Gives trusted options
  • Feels like one smart assistant for wellness planning

How It Makes Money

Four revenue streams

Revenue model

  • Booking commission: keep 15 to 40 percent per booking/order depending on category
  • Premium membership: monthly fee for high-touch support and advanced planning
  • Partner fees: listing, featured placement, and premium partner tooling
  • B2B deals later: company wellness packages
